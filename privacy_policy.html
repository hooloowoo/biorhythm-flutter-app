🛡️ Our Privacy Promise

Your biorhythm data is private, stays on your device, and is never shared with anyone.

📱 About This App

This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app to calculate and analyze your biorhythm cycles.

📊 What Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • 🎂 Birth Date: Required to calculate your physical, emotional, and intellectual cycles
  • 📅 Analysis Dates: Dates you select for biorhythm analysis
  • 🌍 Language Preference: English or Hungarian setting

Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App performance data (crashes, errors)
  • Usage statistics (if analytics enabled)

🎯 How We Use Your Information

✅ What We DO:

  • Calculate biorhythms using your birth date
  • Generate charts and analysis for your selected dates
  • Store data locally on your device only
  • Improve app performance through anonymized usage data

❌ What We DON'T Do:

  • Share your personal data with third parties
  • Store data on external servers or in the cloud
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Sell your information to anyone
  • Require account creation or personal registration

🔐 Data Security & Storage

Local Storage Only

All your personal biorhythm data is stored exclusively on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by iOS and Android. Your birth date and analysis data never leave your phone.

No Cloud Backup

We do not automatically back up your personal data to cloud services. If you want to backup your data, you can use your device's built-in backup features.

👥 Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties. Your biorhythm calculations and birth date remain private and are processed entirely on your device.

Anonymized Data

We may collect anonymized, non-identifiable usage statistics to improve the app, such as:

  • Which features are used most often
  • App crashes and technical issues
  • General usage patterns (without personal data)

🧒 Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The app's educational nature makes it appropriate for learning about biorhythm cycles.

🌍 Your Privacy Rights

You Have the Right To:

  • 👀 View your data: See what information the app stores
  • 🗑️ Delete your data: Remove all information by uninstalling the app
  • ⚙️ Control data collection: Choose what information to provide
  • 📱 Use offline: All features work without internet connection
